# Clinical Validation Program

## 1. Program Objective

Generate the evidence required to support a provider-supervised early cognitive impairment assessment product in Singapore, while simultaneously building the labeled dataset required for the home longitudinal program.

## 2. Study Sequence

### Study A: Workflow Feasibility

**Timing:** April 2026 to September 2026

Purpose:

- prove capture quality
- confirm workflow usability
- validate consent and identity processes
- measure session completion and QC failure modes

Outputs:

- updated protocol
- staff training materials
- capture environment standards

### Study B: Prospective Single-Site Pilot

**Timing:** October 2026 to March 2027

Purpose:

- collect the first proprietary labeled dataset
- benchmark speech-first clinic models
- validate clinician workflow fit

Target cohort:

- healthy controls
- MCI
- mild dementia

### Study C: Expanded Singapore Pilot

**Timing:** April 2027 to September 2027

Purpose:

- increase cohort diversity
- validate calibration
- start repeated home monitoring on a subset

### Study D: Multi-Site External Validation

**Timing:** October 2027 onward

Purpose:

- locked-model validation
- subgroup robustness
- evidence package for the first submission path

## 3. Label Strategy

Each participant should have:

- clinician adjudication
- reference cognitive test scores
- encounter diagnosis or diagnostic category
- follow-up outcome where feasible

Preferred clinic label package:

- clinician-reviewed diagnostic category
- MoCA or equivalent cognitive screen
- relevant neuropsych battery or specialist assessment where available

## 4. Home Monitoring Substudy

Enroll a subset of clinic participants into home monitoring.

Default plan:

- 28-day baseline build window
- 90-day drift observation window
- 6-12 month follow-up where feasible

Minimum home data objective:

- enough repeated sessions to estimate adherence, stability, and early alert burden

## 5. Inclusion Priorities

- older adults representative of intended provider population
- English and Mandarin speakers first
- balanced representation across healthy, MCI, and mild dementia bands

## 6. Exclusion And Flagging

Exclude or separately flag:

- severe acute illness affecting participation
- recording environments with unusable capture quality
- major confounds that invalidate the assessment without separate analysis plans

## 7. Primary Endpoints

### Clinic Product

- sensitivity and specificity against clinician-adjudicated impairment status
- ROC-AUC
- calibration quality
- session usability rate

### Home Product

- baseline completion
- adherence
- alert precision
- lead time before clinically observed change

## 8. Operational Requirements

- all pilot sessions use the shared data contract
- raw artifacts are retained with auditability
- clinician labels are traceable to source records
- protocol deviations are logged
- staff training and site qualification are documented

## 9. Evidence Package Outputs

- study protocol
- case report forms
- site training checklist
- analysis plan
- locked test report
- subgroup analysis report
- clinical evaluation summary
